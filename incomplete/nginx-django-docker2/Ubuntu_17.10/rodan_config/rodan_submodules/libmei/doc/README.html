Overview

LibMEI is a C++ library for reading and writing MEI files. It is developed by the Distributed Digital Music Archives and Libraries Lab at the Schulich School of Music at McGill University, Montreal QC.

LibMEI seeks to define a common software library for interacting with files encoded in MEI. This is targeted at software developers who wish to integrate support for MEI into their software.
